The Best Movies According to Mark

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Matrix

The matrix is a sci-fi movie with cool visual effects and storyline. It is about living in a virtual reality in which people thought is the real world.

Titanic

The movie is based on a real life tragedy that shocked the world. The Titanic was a the biggest ship of its time and once thought as unsinkable. However, during it's first voyage, the ship was damaged severely as it hit an iceberg causing it to sink.

Karate Kid

One of the greatest movie is the Karate Kid. It's greatness does not come from the visual effects but rather from the values it presents to the audience.
